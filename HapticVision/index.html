Good to know

Questions

Will it hurt my eyes or make me dizzy?

No. Depending on the task you'll be seated or walking a short, cleared course, and a researcher stays with you the whole time. Any obstacles are virtual or soft, harmless objects (like cardboard boxes). The simulated vision loss is just a visual effect — it does not affect your real eyes. If you ever feel unwell, we stop immediately.

Do I need any VR or gaming experience?

None at all. We walk you through everything and there's a short practice round before we begin.

Why can't I wear glasses?

Glasses often don't fit comfortably inside the headset and can affect the eye tracking. Contact lenses work perfectly. If you have neither and see well without correction, that's great too.

How and when do I get paid?

Study 1: $30 after Session 1, then $100 once you finish Sessions 2 and 3 (up to $130 total). Study 2: $50 after each of the two sessions ($100 total). Payment method is arranged at your first visit.

How long is each session?

About 1.5 hours, including setup, breaks, and short questionnaires.
